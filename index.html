Names of Cryptocurrencies

Our three specially created cryptocurrencies are inspired by Polish cuisine:

  • Bigos-Coin - Inspired by the traditional Polish dish "Bigos" (hunterâ€™s stew), which reflects richness and variety. Bigos-Coin symbolizes flexibility and the ability to adapt to changing conditions, much like the dish itself that evolves when reheated.
  • Pierog-Coin - Based on "pierogi", small dumplings filled with a variety of fillings, Pierog-Coin represents diversity in the world of cryptocurrencies and innovation in every piece. Just like pierogi, which can be savory or sweet, Pierog-Coin embraces versatility.
  • Kebab-Coin - Though not traditionally Polish, kebab is a popular street food in Poland. Kebab-Coin represents speed and flexibility in cryptocurrency trading. Just like a freshly served kebab, Kebab-Coin reflects the fast-paced nature of crypto trading.

These cryptocurrencies are designed not only to simulate trading but also to add a fun and cultural touch to the digital assets world. With each coin, we aim to bring some Polish flavor to your trading experience!
